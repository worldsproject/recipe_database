What is db.yourdaily.menu?

db.yourdaily.menu is a collection of recipes that you can search. As of now you can search by the existence (or non-existence) of ingredients, or by a full text search that searches titles and directions.

Recipes are returned with an id, allowing easier future lookup, along with ingredient list, directions, origins and other information.
